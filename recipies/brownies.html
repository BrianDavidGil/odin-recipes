Brownies

Bake these easy brownies whenever you need to quickly satisfy your sweet tooth. Made with kitchen staples, you'll come back to this easy brownie recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Walnuts

It could be simpler to throw these quick and easy brownies together on a whim. You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below.

Steps

  1. Place the tortilla chips on a large plate.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter and eggs in a large bowl until combined.
  3. Add and mix the rest of the ingredients cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Decorate with walnut halves.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven about 20 to 30 minutes.
  7. Cool before slicing into squares.
  8. Enjoy it!

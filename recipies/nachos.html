Nachos

Nachos are a popular Mexican dish consisting of tortilla chips typically topped with melted cheese and often accompanied by various toppings such as jalapeños, sour cream, guacamole, salsa, or seasoned meats. They are often served as a snack or appetizer in restaurants, at parties, or as a casual meal.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla chips
  • Refried beans
  • Sliced jalapeños peppers
  • Nacho cheese sauce
  • Shredded chicken or bite-sized pieces of boneless chicken

A basic recipe for nachos with refried beans that can be modified to anything you have on hand!

Steps

  1. Place the tortilla chips on a large plate.
  2. Spread refried beans over the chips.
  3. Layer on chicken and jalapenos.
  4. Top with nacho cheese sauce.
  5. Enjoy it!

Eat immediately for best flavor.

Guacamole

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Roma Tomato
  • Purple Onion
  • Lime
  • Salt & Pepper

This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from fresh cilantro. You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes. Serve with homemade tortilla chips.

Steps

  1. Cut onion and tomato into cubes.
  2. Chop fresh cilantro.
  3. Mash avocados, lime juice, salt and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  4. Mix in tomatoes, onion and cilantro.
  5. Enjoy it!

Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
